ABOUT US

Founded in April 2020 as the COVID-19 Taskforce for Non-Profits, we initially focused on helping Non-Profits adapt and navigate the challenges arising from the pandemic. Beyond COVID-19, we seek to continue to help Non-Profits reimagine how they can continue to THRIVE as resilient and future-ready Non-Profits. To date, we have worked on over 35 pro-bono consulting projects with Non-Profits/Social Enterprises across various sectors, and are looking forward to continue to drive impact alongside our partners!

OUR PROJECTS

We have worked with Lions Befrienders on 2 consulting projects thus far!When COVID-19 first hit, we worked with Lions Befrienders on a strategic planning project to support them in identifying new emerging needs of the elderly and reimagining the eldery engagement journey, to enable the elderly to lead meaningful lives.We subsequently supported Lions Befrienders in volunteer engagement project, identifying key motivations and barriers of potential volunteers, developing a new volunteer lifecycle to engage them at different points of the organization's development.

We worked with Rotary Learning Institute Singapore on a financial sustainability project, helping them to formulate strategies to remain relevant during the COVID-19 situation, developing new course offerings, and outlining their go-to-market strategy.

We worked with Disabled People’s Association Singapore on a corporate donor engagement project, helping them to identify their unique value proposition to attract donors for their new flagship programme that promotes sexuality education for youths with disabilities (DisOrdinary Love).

We worked with Aphasia SG on a beneficiary engagement and public outreach project, helping them to meet unmet beneficiary needs, and design outreach efforts to educate the general public about Aphasia.

We worked with Salvation Army on a strategic planning project, helping them to redefine and strengthen their Unique Value Proposition and programme offerings, with the end goal of engaging corporate donors and volunteers to further enhance their work.
