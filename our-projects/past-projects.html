We worked with Disabled People’s Association Singapore on a corporate donor engagement project, helping them to identify their unique value proposition to attract donors for their new flagship programme that promotes sexuality education for youths with disabilities (DisOrdinary Love).

We worked with Salvation Army on a strategic planning project, helping them to redefine and strengthen their Unique Value Proposition and programme offerings, with the end goal of engaging corporate donors and volunteers to further enhance their work.

We worked with MYMCA on a strategic planning project, redefining their Unique Value Proposition and developing a new flagship programme targeted at helping to shape youths to become social change leaders of tomorrow.

We worked with Ground-Up Initiative (GUI) on a strategic planning project, supporting GUI in articulating its value proposition, and redefining its operating model. We delivered a 3-year business plan which allowed GUI to crystallise its aspiration while achieving financial sustainability.
