PARTNER WITH US

Thrive Social Consulting is always on the lookout for individuals who want to work at the heart of every great change. We seek students and professional volunteers who have a passion for the social sector. We work alongside some of the most influential organisations in the social sector, helping them redefine their operational models and break new ground - creating lasting impact for their beneficiaries, volunteers and donors. If you have new ideas, passion for the social sector and would like to meet other like-minded individuals, volunteer with us today!

DISCOVER HOW YOU CAN GET INVOLVED

Students

Students can volunteer with us as an Engagement Lead or as a Consultant!

Engagement Leads will have the opportunity to lead teams of 3-5 volunteers to manage the overall project direction, drive growth and interface with our partner organisations.

Consultants will have the opportunity to conduct market and internal analysis, develop strategy, and work in a dynamic team.

APPLY HERE

Professionals

Professional volunteers can volunteer with us to mentor the student consultants!

Professional volunteers will mentor our consultants and build their capabilities to help solve Non-Profits' biggest challenges.

We welcome volunteers with experience in the social sector, and skills such as strategy, marketing and stakeholder engagement to join us!
