"Reimagining Singapore's Social Sector - Resilient and Future-Ready" Webinar*

We launched the idea to consolidate our insights from the 10 consulting projects we managed over 2020 as well as 30 interviews with various organizations into one shared resource - to give non-profits a starting point in rethinking beneficiary, donor and volunteer engagement. We launched this through a webinar that was graced by our Guest of Honour, Speaker Tan Chuan-Jin.

We are proud to share this with everyone, and hope it serves as a useful first-step in reimagining the non-profits of tomorrow.

A big thank you to NVPC, Lien Centre for Social Innovation and Hon Liang Yi from Company of Good, we could not have done it without you!
